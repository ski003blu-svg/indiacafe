## Welcome To India Cafe Located In Iowa City Iowa, and Fairfield Iowa.

We started serving our community in 1994. Since then we have provided our customers with delicious food. Our chef prepares every meal in the attempt to bring part of our culture to your plate. Our success in Fairfield gave us the opportunity to bring our culture and our food to Iowa City.
